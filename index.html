Leonard Wong

 Software Engineer @ NCS Group & Graduate Student @ NUS ISS

Summary

Leonard is a Software Engineer at NCS Group and is in NCS's graduate program, Nucleus. Concurrently, he serves as a Brand Ambassador, adeptly promoting the company and representing the brand at various events and forums.
With an unwavering work ethic and a passion for the ICT industry, Leonard continually strives to improve his skills and knowledge. He takes immense pride in producing high-quality work and remains committed to upholding the highest standards of values and vision.
He has co-authored a research paper titled "XSS for the Masses: Integrating Security in a Web Programming Course using a Security Scanner" and completed almost a year of internships, including a six-week overseas internship in Silicon Valley, California, under the MPA Global Internship Award 2019.
Leonard holds a Bachelor's degree in Information Systems (Cyber Security) from Singapore Management University (SMU), under the Quantedge Foundation Scholarship. He also holds a Diploma in Business Informatics with Merit from Nanyang Polytechnic.
