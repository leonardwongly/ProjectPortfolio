Controls

AI assists. Humans approve the risky actions.

The implementation uses least-privilege access, dry-run modes, human approval gates, and redacted logging for sensitive workflows.

No secrets in code

Credentials stay in approved stores or platform-managed connections, not source files.

Explicit scopes

PII minimization

Logs and demos avoid unnecessary personal or sensitive data.

Rollback notes

Owners get instructions to pause, revoke, inspect, and recover the workflow.
